The header tag, or the h1 tag in HTML, will usually be the title of a post, or other emphasized text on the page. It will usually be the largest text that stands out. There are other header tags in HTML too, like an h2, h3, h4, etc

For Example

h1 tag is the header tag of a first tag

h2 tag is the header tag of a Second tag

h3 tag is the header tag of a third tag

h4 tag is the header tag of a fourth tag

h5 tag is the header tag of a fifth tag
h6 tag is the header tag of six tag
